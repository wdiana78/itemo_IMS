About ITEMO IMS

ITEMO IMS is a demo inventory system for construction and stock heavy teams. It focuses on clear records, simple screens, and a clean audit trail from suppliers to project sites.

When to contact us
  • Questions about stock levels and item setup.
  • Issues with supplier or client records.
  • Ideas for new modules, such as requests or payments.
Technical details

ITEMO IMS is built with Django and Bootstrap, using beginner friendly patterns so it is easy to extend.
