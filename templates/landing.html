Who is it for

Construction firms, hardware shops, and any stock heavy team.

What it tracks

Items, suppliers, clients or departments, orders, issues, and payments.

Why it helps

Fewer stock surprises, better planning, and a cleaner audit trail.

Core strengths

Follow material from supplier to site in one simple system.

ITEMO IMS keeps the flow of materials clear from purchase order to delivery to items issued out to departments or project sites. Simple screens, familiar forms, less confusion.

01
Clean stock records

Items, quantities, and values recorded in one place with clear status for low or out of stock.

02
Supplier visibility

Central list of suppliers with contacts and their related purchase orders.

03
Issues and payments

Track what left the store, who received it, and how it was paid for, including M Pesa records.

How it fits your workflow

A simple daily flow your team can actually follow.

Step 1 - Record items and suppliers

Capture your common materials, suppliers, and project clients or departments, once.

Step 2 - Log orders and deliveries

Raise purchase orders and update stock when deliveries arrive at the main store.

Step 3 - Issue items and track payments

Record issues to sites or departments and attach payment details so finance and stores stay aligned.
