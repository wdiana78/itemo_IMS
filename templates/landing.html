Who is it for

Construction firms, hardware stores, clinics, retail outlets, distributors, and any stock heavy operations team that needs clear, reliable inventory data.

What it tracks

Items, categories, suppliers, clients or departments, purchase orders, stock movements, and related payment information in one inventory system.

Why it helps

Fewer stock surprises, better inventory planning, and a clearer audit trail across your operations, from head office to site level.

Core strengths

Follow every item from supplier to project or department in one system.

ITEMO IMS keeps your inventory lifecycle in one connected flow – from purchase order and delivery into store, to items issued out to projects, branches, or departments. The goal is simple screens, familiar forms, and less confusion for the people who actually use the system every day.

01
Clean stock records

Items, quantities, and values recorded in one place with clear indicators for healthy, low, and out-of-stock levels across locations.

02
Supplier visibility

Central supplier list with contact details and linked purchase orders and deliveries, making it easier to follow performance and spend over time.

03
Issues and payments

Track what leaves the store, who received it, and how it was paid for, including mobile money references where applicable, for a clean audit trail.

How it fits your workflow

A simple daily flow your team can actually follow.

Step 1 – Record items and suppliers

Set up your common materials or products, suppliers, and key clients, branches, or departments once, so every transaction in ITEMO IMS uses consistent master data.

Step 2 – Log orders and deliveries

Raise purchase orders and update stock as deliveries arrive at your main store or warehouse, keeping inventory balances aligned with what is physically on the shelf.

Step 3 – Issue items and track payments

Record issues to sites, branches, or departments and attach payment details, so operations and finance share the same view of what was issued, to whom, and how it was settled.

  • Accessible through the browser on laptops and desktops for your whole team.
  • Structured to keep inventory data consistent and easy to navigate.
  • Ready for more modules later – item requests, procurement, payments, and reporting.

Ready to give your inventory a calm home

Start with a few items, suppliers, and one project or department. As your operations grow, you can scale your ITEMO IMS workspace to more users, locations, and modules without losing control of your stock.
