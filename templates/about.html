About ITEMO IMS

Smart inventory for calm, predictable work.

ITEMO IMS is a web based inventory management system designed to help construction and stock heavy teams keep clear, reliable records. Instead of scattered spreadsheets and guesswork, managers get one place to see items, quantities, and stock status.

  • Clean overview of stock levels across items and categories.
  • Simple workflows for recording suppliers, clients, and movements.
  • Built to feel like a real tool, even as a student project demo.
Project snapshot
  • Domain: Inventory for construction and stock heavy businesses.
  • Core entities: Items, suppliers, clients, orders, issues, payments.
  • Features: Authentication, CRUD screens, dashboard statistics.

ITEMO IMS is intentionally scoped so that beginners can understand the code, while still feeling like a tool you could grow into a full enterprise system.

The problem many teams face

Many small and medium teams manage materials using ad-hoc methods: separate spreadsheets, WhatsApp messages, and paper notes. As the work grows, it becomes difficult to answer basic questions with confidence.

  • Unclear which items are low or fully out of stock.
  • Hard to trace what was delivered, to which site, and when.
  • No single place to review supplier performance or pending payments.

How ITEMO IMS helps

  • Central dashboard that highlights totals, low stock, and out-of-stock items.
  • Clear records for suppliers, clients or departments, and their related orders.
  • Simple forms for recording deliveries, issues to sites, and payment status.
  • Cleaner audit trail from supplier, through store, to project sites.

Link to Sustainable Development Goals

This project supports SDG 8 – Decent Work and Economic Growth by reducing waste, interruptions, and last-minute fire-fighting in operations.

  • Better stock visibility helps teams plan ahead, avoiding costly emergency purchases and delays on site.
  • Clearer records make it easier for finance and operations to work together, improving trust and accountability.
  • A structured digital system encourages more formal jobs in inventory, storekeeping, and data-driven decision making.

How the system is organised

ITEMO IMS is broken into clear sections so that both users and developers can reason about the system without feeling lost.

  • Inventory: Items, categories, and quantities on hand.
  • Suppliers: Vendor records, contacts, and related orders.
  • Clients / departments: Projects, sites, or internal units that receive materials.
  • Orders and issues: Tracking what comes into the store and what leaves for the field.
  • Payments: Overview of settled and pending payments against orders.
For store teams
  • Log deliveries quickly.
  • See low stock at a glance.
  • Print or export records later.
For managers
  • Monitor high-value items.
  • Review suppliers and sites.
  • Track pending payments.

In a production rollout, this structure can extend to multiple warehouses, approval flows, and richer reporting without changing the core idea.

Tech stack

  • Backend: Django with standard class-based views and forms.
  • Frontend: Bootstrap 5 plus a custom ITEMO theme.
  • Database: Django’s relational ORM (SQLite in this demo).
  • Auth: Built-in Django authentication and basic access control.

From demo to real deployment

  • Connect to a production database such as PostgreSQL.
  • Add user roles for storekeepers, project managers, and finance.
  • Integrate payment gateways (for example, M-Pesa via Daraja API).
  • Extend reports for monthly summaries and site-level comparisons.
